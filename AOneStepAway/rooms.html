"A One Step Away" is a rustically quaint Bed and Breakfast Cabin House which includes an exquisite antique shopping village located next to the Cabin House.
Sleep in rooms named after the settlers of those who walked the streets before you. Your stay will take you "One Step Away" from the every day rush of those times of the Twenty-first Century.


Jenny Lynn Chamber:

Dedicated to the famous lady of the theater known as the Swedish Nightingale. There was a theater in Idaho City named after this Swedish soprano singer. Private Shower Bathroom.


Miner's Room:

Named for all the men who left their native homes in search of their fortunes and are the ones who first this area. Private Shower and Antique Clawtub Bathroom.


Beth Parkinson Suite:

Dedicated to the long time local resident of Idaho City and once owner of the house that is now named "A One Step Away." This suite is located in the upstairs of the main house and has two rooms. Private Shower and Antique Clawtub Bathroom


Idaho Dredge:

Named after mining equipment used in removing the precious "Gold" from the earth in Idaho City. Private Antique Clawtub Bathroom.
